More about Dancing Through the Decades (DTD) Festival:


Music lovers of all decades, this is for you. Introducing, Dancing Through the Decades (DTD)!! This four day music festival will throw it back to the best decades- the 70s, 80s, 90s, and early 2000s. This is an event full of what we all love the most...nostalgia. Through star-filled lineups, fashions statements, amazing art, and artisanal food, this will be a time to remember. Group your friends together, stretch out your favorite pair of flared jeans sitting in the back of your closet, and have yourself a time. You won't want to miss out.
Are you ready to have the time of your life?

What inspired the Dancing Through the Decades (DTD) Festival?


Everyone loves nostalgia. Whether it be a song we sang to every morning on the drive to school or the clothes we saw in the mall as teenagers. We wanted to bring that back to you beacuase it's always nice to reminisce on our wacky fashion choices, throwback music, and eat delicious food. We are providing all of that for you and more.


We want to share this experience with you, so come and join us!!
