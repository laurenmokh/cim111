Baking Brownies (How-To)

    Ingredients:

  • 1 box of Trader Joeâ€™s Brownie Truffle baking mix
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 stick of butter

    Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Lightly grease pan with butter or cooking spray
  3. Melt butter in microwave for 1 minute
  4. Whisk in eggs until blended
  5. Add brownie mix
  6. Mix all ingredients together until thick paste is made
  7. Spread batter in pan, filling corners
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  9. Allow to cool for another 30 mins
  10. Enjoy!

      11. Tips

    • All ovens cook differently, so make sure to keep an eye out when brownies are cooking in the oven. Check brownies at the minimum baking time by inserting a toothpick in the brownie mix. If the toothpick comes out clean, the brownies are finished. If not, bake for an additional 5 minutes.
    • Enjoy warm brownies with a glass of cold milk!
