Baking Brownies

    Ingredients:

  • 1 box of Trader Joe's Brownie Truffle baking mix
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 stick of butter

    Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Lightly grease pan with butter or cooking spray
  3. Melt butter in microwave for 1 minute
  4. Whisk in eggs until blended
  5. Add brownie mix
  6. Mix all ingredients together until thick paste is made
  7. Spread batter in pan, filling corners
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  9. Allow to cool for another 30 mins
  10. Enjoy!
